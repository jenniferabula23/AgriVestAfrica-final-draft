Agrivest Africa


Agrivest Africa is a farmer-owned and equity crowdfunding platform focused on bridging the gap between investors and agri-value chain. Our goal is to maximize the investors returns and help farmers get good returns while lowering risk.According to a survey conducted by Agri-Value Chain, the number one challenge facing farmers in Africa is access to capital. That's why we created Agrivest Africa—to create passive income for investors, create opportunity for farmers to access funds and maximize production, and create a market place for buyers.

What You Stand to Benefit

As An Agrivestor

Production is more reliable than open-market purchases and also more consistent quality can be obtained than if purchases were made on the open market.



As A Farmer

The prime advantage for farmers is that the sponsor will normally undertake to purchase all produce grown, within specified quality and quantity parameters. Contracts will also provide farmers with access to a wide range of managerial, technical and extension services that otherwise may be unobtainable.




As An Aggregator

The Agrivest platform serves as a trusted hub for the sourcing of farm produce at farm gates prices. As a firm that deals directly with farmers and regulators, buyers are sure to get the quality produce they deserve from us at affordable prices. The Agrivest platform also notify buyers what crops are available at when and at what prices to help them make proper decisions.
